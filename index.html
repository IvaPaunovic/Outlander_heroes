Claire - the Sassenach

About Claire

Claire Beauchamp Randall Fraser is a nurse, later a doctor, and a time-traveler who has lived both in the 20th century and the 18th century.
While on a second honeymoon in Scotland with her husband, Frank Randall, Claire inadvertently travels two hundred years into the past, where she meets and eventually marries Jamie Fraser.
As the primary (and sole first-person) narrator of the Outlander novels, Claire is compassionate but medically ruthless, with a quick tongue that tends to get her into trouble.

Characteristics

  1. Claire is often described as wild and difficult to hold back, but sametime very caring and kind person
  2. Her's unusual upbringing makes her much more independent than most women of her era
  3. Upon her arrival in the 18th century, she stands out for her toughness and outspoken nature
  4. As a nurse and healer, Claire is able to take charge and stay calm in stressful situations
  5. Also stubborn, she often questions orders from others that she doesn't agree with or understand
